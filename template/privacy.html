Privacy Policy


Information about slack channel and team are stored along with member name provided using member-add command. show-config command can be used to see what is the configuratin that is stored by Pair Generator. We use member names to generate random teams of pairs and push it to slack channel everyday at 8 am or when generate command is sent.

All connections between Pair Generator and Slack are made over valid secure connections. We don't store any other information from slack channel or team except what is given using /pair-generator slash command.
